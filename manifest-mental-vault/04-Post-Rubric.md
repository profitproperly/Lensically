# Post Rubric

Score every candidate from 0 to 5 in each category.

## Brand Fit

5: unmistakably Manifest Mental, direct, clean, emotionally specific.
3: usable but somewhat generic.
1: could belong to any affirmation account.

## Performance Promise

5: clear emotional or identity payoff that makes the reader want to like/repost.
3: understandable but not sharp.
1: vague or low-stakes.

## Novelty

5: fresh wording or angle while still on brand.
3: familiar but acceptable.
1: too close to recent posts or archive winners.

## Fatigue Risk

5: low repetition risk.
3: acceptable if balanced by other slots.
1: overuses a current phrase/topic/shape.

## Clarity

5: instantly understood.
3: understandable with mild softness.
1: abstract, clunky, or overexplained.

## Selection Rule

Final posts should generally score at least 21 out of 25. Anything below 18 should be rejected unless the agent explicitly explains why it is being used as a controlled experiment.
