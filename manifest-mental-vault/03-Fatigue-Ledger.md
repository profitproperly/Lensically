# Fatigue Ledger

## Purpose

Track repetition risk. A post can be good and still be wrong for the slate if it increases audience fatigue.

## Watchlist

- repeated openings
- repeated promises
- repeated nouns
- repeated sentence rhythm
- too many money posts in one day
- too many love posts in one day
- too much "chosen" language
- too much "aligned" language
- too many posts with the same emotional payoff

## Current Manual Flags

- The old deterministic planner overuses template-like construction.
- The new agent should treat similarity to recent scheduled posts as a rejection signal.

## Agent Update Rules

After each run, append compact notes here only when a phrase, topic, or structure needs a cooldown.
