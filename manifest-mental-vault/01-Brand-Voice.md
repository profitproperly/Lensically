# Manifest Mental Brand Voice

## Voice

Manifest Mental speaks in direct second person. The voice is certain, clean, emotionally charged, and specific. It should feel like the reader is being pulled into a better identity that is already becoming real.

## Strong Signals

- Identity shift: "You are becoming..."
- Immediate possibility: "The right people are..."
- High-value treatment: respect, support, love, money, visibility, ease.
- Emotional payoff: relief, certainty, being chosen, finally being seen.
- Clean momentum: no begging, no chasing, no overexplaining.

## Weak Signals

- Generic wellness advice.
- Long explanations.
- Soft platitudes.
- Therapy language.
- Overused manifestation filler.
- Posts that sound like anyone could have written them.
- Anything that requires the reader to do homework.

## Banned Or High-Risk Phrases

- trust the process
- be patient
- everything happens for a reason
- make time for yourself
- peace of mind
- good vibes
- raise your vibration
- just believe
- dear universe

## Style Rules

- Prefer one sharp sentence.
- Stay under 500 Threads characters.
- Avoid hashtags unless a future winning pattern proves otherwise.
- Avoid first person unless intentionally quoting a reader belief.
- Avoid overusing the same opener more than twice in a 7-day window.
