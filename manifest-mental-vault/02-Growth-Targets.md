# Growth Targets

## North Star

Reach 1,000,000 followers.

## Near-Term Objectives

- Beat the rolling 14-day average likes per post.
- Beat the rolling 14-day daily follower gain floor.
- Identify repeatable winner structures without making the feed feel repetitive.
- Increase the percentage of posts that clear the account's current median performance.

## Daily Slate Shape

The daily slate has 17 hourly posts from 07:00 through 23:00 ET.

Each slot needs:

- objective
- topic
- bet type
- final post
- why it should win
- fatigue risk
- rejection notes for weaker variants

## Bet Types

- proven winner mutation
- new emotional angle
- high-intensity identity shift
- follower-floor stabilizer
- repost/quote bait without sounding manipulative
- love/money/visibility rotation
- fatigue reset post
