# Content Engine

The goal is not to post more. The goal is to create buyer belief.

## Five Content Buckets

1. Mistakes
Show what beginners are doing wrong.

2. Systems
Explain a repeatable process.

3. Examples
Show a simple scenario or breakdown.

4. Contrarian Takes
Challenge weak advice in the market.

5. Offer Posts
Point to the product or lead magnet.

## Daily Mix

For 10 posts:

- 3 mistakes
- 3 systems
- 2 examples
- 1 contrarian take
- 1 offer post

## Post Templates

Most beginners try to [common mistake]. The better move is [specific move]. This works because [reason].

If I had to build [result] from zero, I would start with [first step], then [second step], then [third step].

The fastest way to validate [idea] is to [simple test]. If nobody responds, fix the offer before building more.

Do not build a product first. Build the buyer path first. Product comes after you know the pain.

One simple online income system: pick one buyer, solve one problem, publish one useful idea daily, send people to one offer.

## Content Rules

Be specific.

Teach one idea at a time.

Avoid fake certainty.

Avoid hype.

Avoid dashes.

Do not use hashtags.

Do not use emojis.

Do not ask vague engagement questions.
