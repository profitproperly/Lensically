# Prompt Pack

## Buyer Research Prompt

Act like a direct response strategist. Help me choose a buyer for a simple digital product. Ask for five buyer options, then score each by pain, urgency, ability to pay, ease of reaching them, and product simplicity.

## Offer Prompt

Turn this buyer problem into five simple digital product offers. Each offer must be specific, useful in one week, and easy to explain in one sentence.

## Content Prompt

Create 20 short posts for this offer. Use these buckets: mistakes, systems, examples, contrarian takes, and soft offer posts. Avoid hype, guarantees, hashtags, emojis, and dashes.

## Landing Page Prompt

Write a simple landing page for this starter product. Include headline, subheadline, bullets, who it is for, who it is not for, what is included, and a clear call to action. Do not promise income.

## Improvement Prompt

Analyze these post results and find patterns. Return winning topics, weak topics, phrases to avoid, formats to repeat, and one product improvement idea.
