## Pain Filter

For each buyer, answer:

- What result do they want?
- What gets in the way?
- What do they already spend money on?
- What do they search for?
- What do they complain about?
- What would save them time this week?

## Good Starter Niches

AI templates for small business owners

Content systems for creators

Client getting scripts for freelancers

Offer building kits for beginners

Automation checklists for solo operators

## Bad Starter Niches

Anything too broad, like wealth or success.

Anything that needs proof you do not have.

Anything that requires regulated financial, legal, or medical advice.

Anything where the buyer has no urgent problem.
